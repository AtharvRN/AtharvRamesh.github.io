About Me

I am a graduate student in ECE at UC San Diego and a researcher in the Trustworthy Machine Learning Lab, advised by Prof. Lily Weng.

My current work focuses on interpretability for deep learning models in medical imaging. More broadly, I am interested in AI safety, reinforcement learning, LLM post-training, and efficient inference.

Previously, I worked as an ML Engineer at Netradyne. I earned my B.Tech. in Electrical Engineering from IIT Hyderabad (EE Dept.). I am actively seeking MLE/Research internships in Summer 2026 and beyond.
